About us

Why we call it "Happiness"

We started The Amazing Life Welfare Society with a simple belief: happiness isn't a mood, it's a set of basic conditions — a child who can read, a family that knows how to stay healthy, a neighbourhood that isn't choking on plastic, a well that still has water in it. Our motto is a promise, not a slogan. Every programme we run is built to move one of those conditions forward for someone in the tricity.

What we work on

Four rays, one sun

Every programme we run falls under one of four pillars — the same four rays in our mark.

Education

Free tuition support, school-kit drives, and reading circles for children who'd otherwise fall through the gaps.

Health Awareness

Community health camps, hygiene workshops, and preventive-care awareness sessions in tricity neighbourhoods.

Environmental Conservation

Tree-plantation drives, plastic-free campaigns, and neighbourhood clean-up efforts across the region.

Water Conservation

Rainwater-harvesting awareness, clean-water access drives, and school workshops on responsible water use.
